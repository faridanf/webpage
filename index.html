Farid Aliniaiefard
I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Mathematics at the University of British Columbia under mentorship of Stephanie van Willigenburg. Previously, I was a Visiting Assistant Professor (Burnett Meyer Postdoc) at the Department of Mathematics at University of Colorado Boulder under mentorship of Nat Thiem . I got my Ph.D. in 2017 under the supervision of Nantel Bergeron in Algebraic Combinatorics at York University, Toronto.
